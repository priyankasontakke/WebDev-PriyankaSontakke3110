BCA


Bachelor of Computer Applications is a latest 4 years of degree course as per NEP–2020 in the computer applications field and a route for science, arts, commerce and MCVC students of 10+2 [MAH-BCA CET Compulsory] to join the group of computer professionals. Bachelor of Computer Applications at MIT College of Computer Science & IT, Basmath relates to designing, development & use of computer applications. Students at MIT College of Computer Science & IT, Basmath learn how to use different applications and also how to bring into play combination of operating system, a utility (performs maintenance or general-purpose chores) and a programming language (by which computer programs are constructed). This helps Bachelor of Computer Applications graduates to prepare documents, presentations, projects, design websites and exhibit skills in effectively running software programs. Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) course aims at providing complete knowledge to the students on diverse areas – numerical and logical methods, networking and internet, programming basics, computer fundamentals, database and application packages. The Bachelor of Computer Applications course also helps students to develop their core competencies in Computer Applications thus, creating the best and skilled professionals for the IT field. If you have a keen interest in computers and possess requisite skills to apply concepts and can analyze the situation very well, Bachelor of Computer Applications is the best course for you. Subjects that are studied in Bachelor of Computer Application are at par with engineering subjects. Computer application professionals learn all aspects of networking, integration, server management, and the use of computers in today’s high-tech savvy world. They learn systems analysis, system programming, software developing, project management and systems management. After graduation, they can even pursue (MCA) Master of Computer Applications master degree program in computer applications. Future Career Prospects Bachelor of Computer Applications graduates can initiate their career in either Private or Government sector as there are a large number of employment opportunities in these sectors. This degree actually helps the students to get a complete knowledge on computer applications so that they can have an ideal launch pad for thriving career in the IT field. The job market for Bachelor of Computer Applications has seen a high jump, due to the extensive use of computers in offices, BPOs, public places, homes and businesses over the years. Therefore, Bachelor of Computer Applications graduates may look forward to rewarding career in several spheres like: system and network administration, web designing, software programming, database administration etc. Eligibility Criteria Those students who have passed 10+2 [MAH-BCA CET] exam with minimum 45% of aggregate marks (40% for SC and ST Candidates) in any stream (Arts/Commerce/Science/MCVC) are qualified to opt for this course. The students who are pursuing higher secondary education assume that only science students can apply for the Bachelor of Computer Applications course. However, the fact is even students from other streams can apply for the Bachelor of Computer Applications course at MIT College of Computer Science & IT, Basmath.
